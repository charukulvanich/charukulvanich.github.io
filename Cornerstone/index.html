We create responsive websites, always with the end user in mind. View our process in creating your customized website below.

Domain Configuration

We kick off the process by gathering insights about your business. We will determine the best domain methology for your website base on your criterias and requirements.

  • Do you require a simple website just to show off your awesome work?
  • Will your website collect user information?
  • Will you be maintaining the website yourself?

These questions will help determine if you require a database, content management system, or a template based hosting solution.

Design & Development

A discussion about branding, aesthetic, and business requirements takes place.

  • How many pages does your website require?
  • What functional elements do you wish to have?
  • What color scheme does your business encompass?

A wireframe or a blueprint is then created to show the architecture of the website. Then we design a mock-up to get your feedback. Finally, we accumulate content and start the development process.

Ready for Launch!

Prior to launch, the site goes through a rigorous quality assurance process to ensure your website performs smoothly across all devices and browsers.

We need make sure your website is responsive for desktop and mobile screens, and functions correctly on older browser versions. After the site passes QA testing and with your final approval, the website goes live!
